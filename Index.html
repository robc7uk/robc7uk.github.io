WHAT THE DUCK?!

North Yorkshire Live Music at its Finest!

With over a decade of professional live music experience each, Rob and Rich are fully equipped to entertain any crowd. Based in York, ‘What the Duck!’ are well versed in travelling across Yorkshire to give unforgettable live performances.

Traditional folk guitar techniques mixed with live loop pedals and some subtle synthesiser/drum machine magic give ‘What the Duck!’ a nostalgic sound with a very small footprint. Make no mistake, this is no usual acoustic 2-piece. You've never heard two people make such a big sound! The songs are fast, loud and impossible not to sing and dance to!
